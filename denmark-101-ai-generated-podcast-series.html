Denmark 101 - AI generated podcast series

May 21, 2025 • Vibek Raj Maurya

The Denmark 101 podcast is a 10-episode series that explores Danish society through engaging dialogue between AI hosts. Each episode is 6–7 minutes long and blends storytelling with practical insight. The entire production was powered using AI tools.

Workflow Overview

The production stack looked like this:

Content Outlining: Google Gemini was used to outline all 10 episodes. Its conversational interface helped refine structure and ensure narrative flow across episodes.

Script Writing: Once outlines were locked in, Gemini was again used to generate the detailed transcripts. The results were surprisingly coherent, needing only minimal edits.

Audio Generation: To convert scripts into audio, I used Notebook LLM. Due to quota limits, I had to switch between multiple Google accounts during this phase.

Thumbnail Generation: For episode artwork and thumbnail visuals, I used OpenAI ChatGPT.
